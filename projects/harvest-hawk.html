Overview

Harvest Hawk is a robotics system designed to monitor agricultural conditions through integrated sensors and control systems. The project focused on combining multiple subsystems into a single functional platform capable of real-world operation.

This project demonstrates system-level engineering by integrating sensors, electrical systems, and control logic into a cohesive robotic platform.
